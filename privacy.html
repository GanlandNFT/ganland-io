PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GANLAND ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the GANLAND platform, including ganland.io, ganland.ai, and related services (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Social login credentials (X/Twitter handle, Privy authentication data)
  • Wallet Information: Public wallet addresses associated with your account
  • Content: Prompts, artwork, and other content you create or upload
  • Communications: Messages you send through our platforms or to our support channels

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, interactions with the GAN agent
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP addresses, access times, error logs
  • Blockchain Data: Public transaction data associated with your wallet addresses

Important: We do NOT collect or store your private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords. These remain solely in your control.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process your requests and transactions
  • Generate AI artwork based on your prompts
  • Deploy and manage smart contracts on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We may share information in the following circumstances:

Recipient Purpose
Service Providers Third parties that help us operate (hosting, analytics, AI providers)
Blockchain Networks Transaction data is publicly recorded on-chain
Legal Authorities When required by law or to protect rights and safety
Business Transfers In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Blockchain & Public Data

Please be aware that:

  • Blockchain transactions are public and permanent
  • Your wallet address and transaction history are visible to anyone
  • NFTs you mint or purchase are publicly associated with your wallet
  • We cannot delete or modify data recorded on public blockchains

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our Services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

You may request deletion of your account data, subject to legal retention requirements. Blockchain data cannot be deleted.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure development practices

However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own credentials and devices.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Services integrate with third-party providers including:

  • Leonardo AI: For AI image generation
  • Privy: For authentication and embedded wallets
  • Alchemy: For blockchain infrastructure
  • Supabase: For database services
  • Vercel: For hosting

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (with exceptions)
  • Object to certain processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us via @GanlandNFT on X.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us.

10. International Users

Our Services are operated from the United States. If you access our Services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US or other countries with different data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us on X/Twitter:

  • @GanlandNFT

By using GANLAND, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
